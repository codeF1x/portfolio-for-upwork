Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the collaboration process.

payments

How do payments work?

For most projects on Upwork, we use the Escrow system. You fund milestones before I start, and funds are released only when you approve the work.

restart_alt

What if I need revisions?

Each milestone typically includes 2 rounds of revisions. We will clarify expectations in the Discovery phase to minimize major changes later.

forum

How do we communicate?

Primary communication happens via Upwork Messages. For larger projects, we can schedule weekly Zoom calls to discuss progress.

calendar_month

What is the typical timeline?

Timelines vary by complexity. A landing page might take 3-5 days, while a full website often takes 2-4 weeks. I'll provide a schedule in the brief.
